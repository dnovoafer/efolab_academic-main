After a rigorous international review process, the Scientific Advisory Board of Ikerbasque has unanimously agreed to offer a permanent Research Associate Professor position to David! His appointment, that recognizes his excellent professional trajectory, as well as the interesting research directions his group is pursuing, will begin next April. Fantastic news and boost to keep pushing the frontiers of guided photonics!
