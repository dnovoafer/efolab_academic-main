David has been recognized as OPTICA Senior Member in the [2025 cohort](https://www.optica.org/membership/distinguished_honorary/senior/senior_member_classes/2025_optica_senior_members/). He is the only representative of a Spanish institution among the 72 awardees. Honoured and proud of this remarkable achievement! A small interview (in Spanish) on this occassion can be found [here](https://www.ehu.eus/es/web/campusa/-/la-sociedad-americana-de-optica-distingue-al-doctor-david-novoa-por-sus-aportaciones-en-ese-campo)
